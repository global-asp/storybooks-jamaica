ድመቴ የታለች?

Where is my cat?

We mi kyat de?

አልጋው ስር ነች?

Is it under the bed?

Im anda di bed?

ቁም ሳጥኑ ላይ ነች?

Is it on top of the cupboard?

Im pan tap a di kobad?

ከሶፋው ጀርባ ነች?

Is it behind the couch?

Im de bak a di kouch?

ከቆሻሻ ቅርጫቱ ጎን ነች?

Is it next to the bin?

Im de said a di robish pan?

ቅርጫቱ ውስጥ ነች?

Is it inside the basket?

Im de ina di baaskit?

ከቤቱ ውጪ ነች?

Is it outside the house?

Im de outa duor?

እዚህ ነች!

Here it is!

Si im ya!

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Mezemir Girma
Language: Amharic
Level: Level 1
